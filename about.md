# About

Jie Pang is a lacquer artist, material thinker, and civilization narrator.

His practice centers on lacquer as both material and philosophy.  
Through painting, writing, and digital image-making, he investigates how surfaces preserve memory, how materials embody time, and how civilizations articulate permanence through form.

Based in Alberta and working across artistic, theoretical, and cross-media fields, Pang develops lacquer not only as an artistic technique, but as a structural language of culture, history, and ontology.
